The Company

Evergreen Exports has remained at the forefront of the marine food industry as one of the leading exporters of Fresh & Frozen Sea Food Products. Since its inception in 2005,it has grown into one of the main processing companies in frozen seafood in Cochin. As a trusted name in the marine food industry, we focus on the quality of the products. We are committed to quality and our experts ensure the quality and reliability of our products. 

Integrity and social responsibility form our guiding force. We ensure to offer an entire spectrum well processed, hygienically packed and delivered on time seafood items. We focus on sourcing only high quality products from the sea catch & culture pond to maximize our clients’ satisfaction. The seafood we export remains fresh & retains the flavour & nutritive quality as the manufacturing is done under extremely hygienic conditions and the supervision of thoroughly trained Quality Management personnel. All our products are scientifically tested and manufactured with pioneering technology and innovative packing.

Why Choose Us?
Our quality offerings have enabled us to attract maximum number of customers on international level belonging to food industry, hotel industry, restaurant and retail chain etc., due to following reasons:

Guaranteed freshness and flavour of seafood.

Safe, hygienic and on time product delivery.

Ensured nutritive content.

Effective packaging.

Competitive prices.
